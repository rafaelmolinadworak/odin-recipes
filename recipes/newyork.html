New York Strip Steak Recipe

What will you accomplish with this recipe?

A juicy New York Strip Steak pouring beef juice all over the plate, tender, and ready to be devoured.

Ingredients

  • New York Strip Steak
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Grass fed butter
  • Garlic cloves
  • Fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Season the strip steak on both sides with sea salt and pepper. Do not be afraid of salt or pepper when seasoning the Strip Steak; Make sure there is salt all over every square centimeter of the meat. There is nothing worse than unseasoned food, nothing, so season it well.
  2. Next, add the oil to a large frying pan or cast iron skillet over high heat until it begins to smoke lightly.
  3. Place the steaks into the pan and immediately turn to medium-high heat. Be sure not to crowd the pan to sear the steak, not steam it.
  4. Add garlic cloves, fresh thyme or rosemary, and unsalted butter
  5. Cook the steaks for 2 minutes before moving them around the pan to ensure it becomes browned on every square millimeter of the steak.
  6. After 5-6 minutes of cooking, flip the steak over and repeat the process.
  7. Remove the steak after 2-3 minutes of cooking on the other side and set it on a cutting board or plate for 3-4 minutes to rest for a medium rare internal temperature.
