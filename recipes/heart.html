Beef Heart Steak Recipe

What will you accomplish with this recipe>

A beef heart steak waiting to be devoured by one that will benefit from it's rich nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp of ghee
  • 4 slices of beef heart 1" thick
  • 2 Tbsp rosemary infused extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate heart in apple cider vinegar for 24 hours before cooking, to remove strong blood odor.
  2. Take heart slices from the marinade and pat dry.
  3. Heat a cast iron skillet with the ghee on a high flame for 2/3 minutes.
  4. Lay meat in skillet, temperature should be highly enough to sizzle.
  5. Cook for five minutes on each side until nicely browned on the outside but still pink in the middle.
  6. Drizzle with the rosemary infused extra virgin olive oil.
  7. Serve with bread of choice
